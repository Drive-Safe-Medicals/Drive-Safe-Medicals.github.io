CRANE OPERATOR MEDICALS

At Drive Safe Medicals we provide medical assessments for lifting equipment operators. Our goal is to screen all existing and potential
lifting equipment operators for fitness before and during their employment.

We also recommend renewing an assessment after a short or long employee absence. It is important to recognise that if a GP signs a
worker off as fit to return to work, this may not be the same as fitness to operate a lifting equipment. We can provide confirmation of
fitness to operate in these circumstances.

OUR STANDARD CRANE OPERATOR MEDICAL COSTS £65

WHY CHOOSE US FOR YOUR CRANE OPERATOR MEDICAL?

  • Up to 60% discount on crane operator medicals as compared to your local GP
  • Face to face appointments with highly skilled GMC registered doctors
  • Open 7 days a week
  • Flexible appointments with evening and weekend availability
  • On-site clinics accepted
  • Group bookings accepted
  • Fitness certificates will be sent within 24 hours of your medical assessment (subject to passing the medical assessment)

THE STANDARD CRANE OPERATOR MEDICAL INVOLVES:

  • A series of questions about your medical history
  • Height and weight measurement
  • Blood pressure check
  • Eye test

WHAT HAPPENS AFTER I HAVE MADE A BOOKING?

Once you have successfully booked and paid for your appointment. You will be sent a confirmation email of the details of the
location, date, and time of the appointment, please read these carefully

WHAT SHOULD I BRING TO THE APPOINTMENT?

  1. Photo ID – Driving licence/Passport
  2. Your driving glasses and your glasses prescription if you wear glasses (not contact lenses) to meet the eyesight standard
    needed for driving
  3. Your medication prescription

For group bookings or enquiries about workplace lifting equipment medicals, please contact us on admin@drivesafemedicals.co.uk
