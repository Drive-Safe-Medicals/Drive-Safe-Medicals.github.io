PRE-EMPLOYMENT MEDICALS

To adhere to good occupational health practice, we provide medical assessments to ensure that prospective and employed staff are physically
and psychologically capable of doing the work proposed.

We also provide professional pre-employment medicals to screen individuals for potential risk factors that may restrict them in providing safe
and efficient performance at work. We ensure that the potential employee is not at any health risk, ultimately reducing potential injuries in the
workplace. We also help businesses establish whether any employee adjustments need to be made during the recruitment process.
