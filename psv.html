Public Service Vehicles (PSV) Medicals

Call Now Book Online

We provide D4 medicals for PSV, bus and minibus driving. At your medical appointment, our skilled
doctors will go through a questionnaire about your medical history. A blood pressure check and a
vision test are also included in your appointment.

The medical assessment is carried out by one of our GMC registered doctors

Depending on the council you will be applying to, the doctor will ask you a series of health-related
questions, assess your vision and check your blood pressure. This is to determine whether you meet
the standards to drive a private hire or hackney carriage. The doctor will inform you of your result at
your appointment.

OUR STANDARD TAXI MEDICAL COSTS £55

PLEASE BRING THE FOLLOWING TO YOUR APPOINTMENT:

  1. The relevant council medical form
  2. Identification documents – Driving licence, Passport, one utility bill (gas, electric, telephone,
    water) or bank statement
  3. Your driving glasses and your glasses prescription if you wear glasses (not contact lenses) to
    meet the eyesight standard needed for driving
  4. Your medication prescription
  5. Your medical records
